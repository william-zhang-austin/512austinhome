## Why Buy in Cedar Park?

Cedar Park is one of the best places to live in the Austin metro for families. It offers an exceptional combination of affordable housing, top-rated schools, and suburban quality of life — all within a 25-minute drive of downtown Austin.

### Schools

Cedar Park is served primarily by Leander ISD, which consistently ranks among the top school districts in Central Texas. Vista Ridge High School and Cedar Park High School both have strong academic programs and competitive athletics.

### Housing

Cedar Park has a healthy mix of established neighborhoods and new construction. You'll find:

- **Established neighborhoods** (Twin Creeks, Buttercup Creek, Ranch at Cypress Creek) — Mature trees, larger lots, $400K-$600K
- **New construction** (Bryson, Northside at Leander) — Modern floorplans, energy-efficient, $350K-$550K
- **Townhomes and condos** — Starting around $250K for more affordable entry

### Commute

Cedar Park residents benefit from easy access to US-183, which connects directly to Austin's tech corridor (Apple, Indeed, PayPal offices). The average commute to downtown Austin is 25-35 minutes depending on traffic.

### Things to Do

- **H-E-B Center at Cedar Park** — Home of the Austin Spurs (NBA G League) and Texas Stars (AHL hockey)
- **Brushy Creek Trail** — 8+ miles of hike and bike trails
- **1890 Ranch & Lakeline Mall** — Major shopping and dining
- **Lake Travis** — 20 minutes west for boating, swimming, and waterfront dining
