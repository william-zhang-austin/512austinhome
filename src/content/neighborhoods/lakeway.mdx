## Why Buy in Lakeway?

Lakeway is Austin's premier lakeside community. If you want Hill Country views, lake access, top-rated schools, and a resort-like lifestyle, Lakeway delivers. It's a 30-minute drive to downtown Austin but feels like a completely different world.

### Lake Travis Living

Lake Travis is the crown jewel of the Austin area. Lakeway residents enjoy:

- **Waterfront access** — Public and private boat ramps, marinas, and lake parks
- **Water activities** — Boating, jet skiing, paddleboarding, swimming, fishing
- **Waterfront dining** — The Oasis, Sundancer Grill, and dozens of lakeside restaurants
- **Lake views** — Many Lakeway homes have panoramic lake and Hill Country views

### Schools

Lake Travis ISD is among the best in Texas. Lake Travis High School is a Blue Ribbon School with exceptional academics, athletics (state championship football program), and extracurriculars. Families move to Lakeway specifically for this school district.

### Housing

Lakeway's housing ranges from accessible to ultra-luxury:

- **Condos and townhomes** — Starting around $350K-$450K, great for empty nesters or weekend getaways
- **Single-family homes** — $500K-$1.2M for established neighborhoods (Lakeway proper, Rough Hollow)
- **Waterfront estates** — $1M-$3M+ for Lake Travis waterfront with private docks
- **Golf course homes** — Flintrock Falls, Hills of Lakeway, $700K-$1.5M

### Lifestyle

- **Lakeway Resort & Spa** — Full-service resort with pools, spa, and restaurants
- **Flintrock Falls Golf Club** — Jack Nicklaus-designed course
- **Hamilton Pool Preserve** — One of Texas's most beautiful natural swimming holes (nearby)
- **Bee Cave / Hill Country Galleria** — Shopping and dining 10 minutes away
