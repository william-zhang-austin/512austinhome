## Why Buy in Pflugerville?

Pflugerville is the Austin metro's best-kept secret for value. It offers lower prices than Austin, Cedar Park, or Round Rock while maintaining easy access to major employers and downtown Austin. For first-time buyers and families on a budget, it's hard to beat.

### Affordability

With a median home price around $380,000, Pflugerville is significantly more affordable than Austin proper. You get more square footage for your dollar, and many neighborhoods offer new construction in the $300K range — a price point that's nearly impossible to find in central Austin.

### Location

Despite feeling suburban, Pflugerville is centrally located:

- **Downtown Austin:** 20-30 minutes via I-35 or 130 Toll
- **Domain/tech corridor:** 15-20 minutes
- **Austin-Bergstrom Airport:** 25 minutes via 130 Toll
- **Round Rock/Dell:** 15 minutes

### Housing Options

- **Established neighborhoods** (Falcon Pointe, Blackhawk, Meadows of Blackhawk) — Mature communities, $350K-$500K
- **New construction** (Easton Park adjacent, Whisper Valley) — Some of Austin's most affordable new homes, $280K-$400K
- **Luxury developments** (Sorento, Avalon) — Premium options, $500K-$650K

### Community

Pflugerville has invested heavily in parks, trails, and community amenities:

- **Lake Pflugerville** — 180-acre lake with swimming, kayaking, and a 3-mile trail
- **Pflugerville Pfarmers Market** — Weekly community market
- **Stone Hill Town Center** — Major shopping and dining hub
- **German heritage festivals** — Pflugerville's roots show in annual events like Deutschen Pfest
