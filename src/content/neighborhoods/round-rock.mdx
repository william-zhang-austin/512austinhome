## Why Buy in Round Rock?

Round Rock consistently ranks among the best places to live in Texas. It combines suburban affordability with urban amenities, making it ideal for families, professionals, and anyone who wants good value in the Austin metro.

### Schools

Round Rock ISD is one of the highest-rated districts in Central Texas. Westwood High School is a Blue Ribbon School with nationally competitive academic and STEM programs. The district serves over 50,000 students across multiple high schools.

### Housing

Round Rock offers strong value compared to Austin proper:

- **Established neighborhoods** (Brushy Creek, Behrens Ranch, Forest Creek) — Well-maintained, mature communities, $380K-$600K
- **New developments** (Siena, Headwaters, Paloma Lake) — Modern construction, community amenities, $350K-$500K
- **Luxury homes** (Cat Hollow, Arbor Place) — Larger lots, premium finishes, $600K-$800K+

### Major Employers

Dell Technologies is headquartered here, and the 183/I-35 tech corridor provides easy access to Apple, Amazon, and other major employers. Baylor Scott & White and St. David's medical centers are also major employers.

### Recreation and Community

- **Dell Diamond** — Home of the Round Rock Express (AAA baseball)
- **Old Settlers Park** — 570 acres with sports fields, trails, and a fishing lake
- **Round Rock Premium Outlets** — Major shopping destination
- **Downtown Round Rock** — Historic Main Street with local restaurants and shops
- **Kalahari Resorts** — America's largest indoor waterpark resort
